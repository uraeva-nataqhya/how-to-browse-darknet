## What is the dark&#8239;net?
The dark&#8239;net refers to encrypted online networks accessible only through specialized software, like Tor. Unlike the surface web (e.g., Google-searchable sites), it uses .onion domains for anonymity. It's often associated with privacy tools, whistleblowing platforms, and forums, but also harbors illicit markets, hacking services, and more. ## Tools Needed to Access the dark&#8239;net

### 1. Tor Browser
The gold standard for dark&#8239;net access. Download from the official Tor Project website (torproject.org). It routes traffic through multiple nodes for anonymity.

Use Tails OS (a live USB system) for ultimate privacy. It routes all traffic through Tor and leaves no traces on your device.
